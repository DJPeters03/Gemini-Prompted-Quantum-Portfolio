Step 2: The Problem with the Box

Why the classical prediction went to infinity.
The Missing Link: Before Planck could fix the problem, we must understand why the UV catastrophe happened.

Imagine the black body furnace as a simple 1D box. Light inside can only exist as standing waves (modes) that fit perfectly between the walls. Classical physics assumed every possible wave mode gets an equal share of heat energy.

Current Mode (n): 1 Low Frequency (Red)

Interaction: Slide to increase frequency. Notice how crowded the waves become.

The Catastrophe: In classical physics, n=1 gets $k T$ energy. n=2 gets $k T$ energy. n=50 gets $k T$ energy. Since n goes to infinity (UV), total energy goes to infinity.

Next Step: Introduce Planck's Resonators to limit energy flow into high 'n' modes.
